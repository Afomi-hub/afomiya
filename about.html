About me

Hi, I'm Afomiya Fikadu, an artist at heart and a dreamer by nature. Living in the beautiful landscapes of Ethiopia, I find inspiration in the world around me, translating emotions and stories into my artwork. Art isn't just a hobby for me—it's my passion, my way of expressing thoughts beyond words.

When I'm not creating, you’ll likely find me indulging in one of my favorite pastimes: sleep. There's something about rest that fuels creativity, allowing my imagination to wander freely. I also enjoy getting lost in books, with Jacqueline by Agatha Christie being one of my all-time favorites.

Thank you for visiting my page!
